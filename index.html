Why Choose Us?

Welcome to Sivanmalai Murugan Bus Service, proudly based out of Kangayam. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality daily transit services for our community. Whether you are commuting for work or traveling to see family, we ensure a smooth, comfortable, and timely ride.

Under the management of our proprietor, Revanth, we maintain strict safety protocols and schedule adherence so you can always rely on us.
